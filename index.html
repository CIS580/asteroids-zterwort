Play Asteroids

Instructions

  • Move your ship with W, A, S, D. (Up, Down, Left, Right)
  • You start with 3 lives
  • Shoot lasers with the J key, and each asteroid destroyed awards you with 10 points!
  • Avoid the asteroids, or you will lose a life!
  • Losing a life with place you in the middle of the screen, and INVINCIBLE for 3 seconds, turning your ship blue!
  • If you feel like your in danger, you can press the K key to teleport somewhere randomly, which will apply invincibility.
